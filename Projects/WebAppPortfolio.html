About This Project

Technology Used:
Adobe XD for wireframes and prototypes.

Features:
A clean, minimalistic design with simple navigation, responsive layout, clear section structure (Home, About, Portfolio, Contact), bright and friendly colour palette, and a smooth visual showcase of wireframes, mood board, and final UI design.

Target Audience:
All ages and genders, users with basic computer literacy, recruiters and hiring managers, and especially people from design or web development companies who want to understand my skill set, background, and portfolio quickly.

Challenges Faced:
Balancing creativity with professionalism, keeping colours bright but still readable, maintaining consistent brand identity, ensuring responsive design across screens, organising content clearly, and converting Adobe XD designs accurately into coded layouts.
